# Drilling Daily Report Excerpt

## Well
Offset well `DRL-17H`

## Summary
The well drilled 2,180 ft over the last 24 hours. Average ROP improved in the middle interval after a bit change, but vibration increased when the team pushed weight on bit above the offset recommendation. Two short connection delays were observed. Hole cleaning was acceptable, though drag trended upward near TD.

## Issues Observed
- Elevated stick-slip during the morning section
- MSE increased during the final 400 ft
- Torque spikes during slide intervals

## Actions Taken
- Reduced rotary speed for one interval
- Adjusted weight on bit to stabilize vibration
- Circulated bottoms up before the final section

## Lessons Learned
The offset suggests that aggressive parameter changes improved short-term ROP but increased dysfunction risk. Future wells in the same pad should treat this interval as a trade-off zone between speed and stability.
