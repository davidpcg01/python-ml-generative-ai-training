# Well Log Interpretation Note

## Interval Summary
The target interval shows alternating clean sand and shaly streaks with moderate resistivity separation. Gamma ray remains relatively low in the cleaner sections, while neutron-density crossover is locally consistent with gas effect. Porosity appears strongest in the upper interval, but thin-bed complexity increases uncertainty in the lower section.

## Working Interpretation
The interval is likely composed of stacked channel sands with variable shale content. Net reservoir quality is highest in the upper package. The lower package may remain productive, but should be treated cautiously because thin beds and shale laminations may complicate completion placement.
