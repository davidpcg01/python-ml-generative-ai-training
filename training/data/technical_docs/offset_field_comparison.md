# Offset Field Comparison Note

## Case Comparison
Field Alpha delivered stronger initial rates than Field Beta, but decline steepened after month 10 as water cut increased faster than expected. Field Beta started lower yet showed more stable uptime and a flatter decline profile after intervention work.

## Operational Difference
Field Alpha emphasized aggressive drawdown and larger choke settings early in life. Field Beta used more conservative choke management and a slower ramp.

## Practical Lesson
Initial outperformance did not guarantee better medium-term stability. The comparison suggests that drawdown strategy should be reviewed alongside completion quality and surveillance discipline rather than judged only on early production.
