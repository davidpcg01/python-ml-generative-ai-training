# Reservoir Surveillance Note

## Asset Summary
Pad B wells showed stronger-than-expected liquid decline over the last two months. Three wells remain above plan, but two wells are now trending below the expected oil-rate envelope. Water cut increased most sharply in `WELL_08` and `WELL_10`, while uptime deteriorated in `WELL_09` due to intermittent ESP trips.

## Engineer Interpretation
The recent decline does not appear to be purely reservoir-driven. Operating interruptions and higher water production are likely contributing to the current performance gap.

## Suggested Follow-Up
- review artificial lift history for `WELL_09`
- compare choke management across the pad
- prioritize surveillance on wells with rising water cut and falling uptime
